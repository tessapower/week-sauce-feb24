# Holy Whac-a-Moley!

![Holy Whac-a-Moley](docs/holy-whac-a-moley.gif)

Grab your mallet, hold it tight, and be prepared for the mole whacking experience
of your life! A whac-a-mole arcade game with fun power-ups, multiple environments,
and unique mallets — made in a week for the [Week Sauce](https://itch.io/jam/weeksauce)
game jam (February 2024).

[![Play on itch.io](https://img.shields.io/badge/Play%20on-itch.io-FA5C5C?style=for-the-badge&logo=itchdotio&logoColor=white)](https://sasspower.itch.io/holy-whac-a-moley)

## Gameplay

Moles pop up from their holes — click to whack them before they claw you! Defeat
moles to earn XP and level up, but be careful: as you grow stronger, the moles
spawn faster. Collect health potions and power-ups to survive as long as you can.

### Features

- 3 themed environments, each with unique moles and mallets
- XP and leveling system with increasing difficulty
- Power-ups: health potions, swiftness, colossal strength
- Rare golden moles worth bonus points
- Original pixel art and animations
